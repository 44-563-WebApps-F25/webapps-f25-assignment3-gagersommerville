Job Instructions and TV Shows

  1. Identify the nearest stud
    1. If it is not showing, cut out a piece in the center of the stud
  2. Then cut a piece using a drywall knife that fits the hole
    1. Place the piece into the hole and screw it in
  3. Place drywall tape on the outside of the hole
  4. Cover it in drywall mud
    1. Smooth out the mud
    2. Let it dry and sand it
  5. Lastly, paint it the matching color as the wall
